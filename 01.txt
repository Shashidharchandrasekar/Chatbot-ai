Dr. G.R. Damodaran College of Science, also known as GRD College, is an ISO 9001:2015 certified institution in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, India. It is affiliated to the Bharathiar University and offers undergraduate, postgraduate, M.Phil and Ph.D programmes in Management, Computer Science and Information Technology, Biotechnology, Visual and Mass Communication, Commerce and International Business, Tamil literature and Psychology etc.

The college was established in 1988 and has since grown to become one of the leading colleges in Coimbatore. It is known for its excellent academic infrastructure, experienced faculty and strong industry connections.

GRD College has been accredited with an 'A' grade by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC). It is also approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) for its MBA and MCA courses.

The college has a vibrant campus life with a variety of clubs and societies. It also hosts a number of cultural and sporting events throughout the year.
